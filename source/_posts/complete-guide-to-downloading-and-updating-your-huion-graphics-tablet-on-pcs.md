1. Go to **[AMD’s support page](https://www.amd.com/en/support)**  .
2. Scroll to the search section. Then, select**Graphics** \>**AMD Radeon 6000 Series** \>**AMD Radeon 6900 Series** \>**AMD Radeon RX 6900 XT** , and click**Submit** .
